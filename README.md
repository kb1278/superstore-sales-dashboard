# Superstore Sales Dashboard (Excel)

## Overview

This project presents an interactive Excel dashboard built using the Superstore dataset. The dashboard analyses sales performance, profitability, and customer segments to provide actionable business insights through data visualisation and KPI reporting.

The dashboard was developed using PivotTables, PivotCharts, slicers, and custom KPI cards to enable dynamic exploration of business performance.

---

## Objectives

- Analyse overall sales and profit performance
- Identify the most profitable product categories
- Understand customer segment contributions
- Highlight top-performing states
- Create an interactive dashboard for business decision-making

---

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Excel
- PivotTables
- PivotCharts
- Slicers
- KPI Cards
- Data Visualisation
- Business Analysis

---

## Dashboard Features

### Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

- Total Sales
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Average Discount

### Visualisations

- Sales by Category
- Profit by Category
- Sales by Segment
- Top 10 States by Sales

### Interactive Filters

- Category
- Segment
- Region

All charts and KPIs update dynamically through slicer selections.

---

## Key Insights

- Technology generated the highest sales and profit among all product categories.
- Furniture achieved strong sales but significantly lower profitability compared with other categories.
- The Consumer segment contributed the largest share of total sales.
- California was the highest-performing state by sales revenue.
- Average discount levels may be impacting overall profitability.
